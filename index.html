FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use for 3D printing?
We work with a variety of materials including PLA, PETG, ABS, TPU, and Carbon Fiber composites. Each material is selected based on your specific requirements for strength, flexibility, and finish.
How long does it take to complete an order?
Most orders are shipped within 48 hours. Complex custom projects may take 3-5 days depending on the design complexity and material requirements.
Can I request custom colors?
Yes! We offer a wide range of colors and can even match specific color codes. You can select from our standard palette or request custom colors through our product options.
What is your precision tolerance?
We achieve ±0.05 mm accuracy with layer heights down to 0.1 mm. This precision level ensures your parts fit and function exactly as designed.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by destination. Contact us via WhatsApp for a shipping quote to your location.
